Shinkansen (Bullet Train)
High-speed / dedicated track / intercity backbone
What it is
Japan's high-speed network on dedicated tracks. Fastest trains in the country — minimal stops, high frequency, highly reliable.
Use for
City-to-city movement across long distances. Replaces flights for most Japan itineraries and sets the pace of the trip.
Key routes
Tokyo ↔ KyotoTokyo ↔ OsakaTokyo ↔ HiroshimaTokyo ↔ Kanazawa
Default choice when both endpoints are major cities
■
Limited Express
Regional fast trains / standard track / scenic reach
What it is
Faster regional trains — not high-speed, but limited stops. Run on standard rail lines, not dedicated Shinkansen tracks.
Use for
Destinations off the bullet train network. This is where the itinerary becomes experiential — rural, scenic, and cultural access.
Enables
TakayamaKiso ValleyHakoneKinosaki OnsenHakubaMatsuyamaHokkaido
If Shinkansen = backbone, Limited Express = reach and depth
■
JR Rapid / Local
Commuter / regional connectors / short range
What it is
Standard commuter or regional trains. More stops, slower, lower cost than Limited Express.
Use for
Short-distance connections, final-mile transfers, and close-range city hops.
Examples
Osaka → HimejiTokushima → Takamatsu
Supporting role only — not a primary planning tool for luxury itineraries

Romancecar — Hakone
Specialty premium train / 3 booking paths / easy upsell
What it is
Premium branded train on the Shinjuku ↔ Hakone-Yumoto route. Panoramic windows, reserved seating, elevated comfort.
Outbound
Shinjuku → Hakone-Yumoto: book LTD Express + Romancecar surcharge as two separate line items ($60 + $30).
Return
Hakone-Yumoto → Shinjuku: single all-in ticket ($94.50). No separate surcharge needed.
Bundles
Romancecar + 2-Day or 3-Day Hakone Free Pass available as packaged products — strongest upsell option.
Lead with the experience — advisors sell the Romancecar, not the line items

Quick-reference definitions
Shinkansen
High-speed, long-distance rail connecting major cities; primary transportation backbone
Limited Express
Regional fast trains connecting secondary destinations not served by Shinkansen
JR Rapid / Local
Short-distance or commuter trains for local connections and final-mile access
Airport Connection
Dedicated rail to/from airport — NRT (Narita Express) and KIX (LTD Express) only; no HND train loaded
Romancecar
Specialty premium train Shinjuku ↔ Hakone-Yumoto — outbound is surcharge add-on, return is all-in ticket
Hakone Free Pass
Area pass covering unlimited travel within Hakone — 2-day or 3-day, from Odawara or Shinjuku
Combination Route
Single booking using multiple train types — e.g., Tokyo → Hakodate (Shinkansen + Local Train)
